CoverMap

Track where you've been or plan where you want to go.

About

CoverMap is a simple Android app that lets you draw colored highlights on a world map. Mark the places and regions you've visited, or plan your next route. Add photos you've taken directly to the place you took them.

Features

  • Draw highlights directly on an interactive map
  • Customize color, opacity, and brush size
  • Erase individual strokes with the stroke eraser
  • See what percentage of the area you've covered
  • Sync your highlights to Google Drive
  • Attach photos from Google Photos to map locations
  • Offline map tile caching

Data & Privacy

Your highlights are stored locally and optionally synced to your own Google Drive. Your added photos are stored as thumbnails locally and synced to your own Google Drive. No personal data is collected by the app. Read the full Privacy Policy.
